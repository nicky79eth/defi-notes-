# AMM Design

Automated Market Makers (AMMs) are core components of decentralized exchanges.

They replace traditional order books with algorithmic pricing based on liquidity pools.

## Core Components

- Liquidity Pool
- Token Pair
- Pricing Algorithm
- Trading Fee

## Pricing Model

Most AMMs use the constant product formula:

x * y = k

Where:

x = reserve of token A  
y = reserve of token B  
k = constant value

This formula ensures that liquidity is always available for traders.

## Liquidity Providers

Users deposit token pairs into pools and receive LP tokens representing their share.

LPs earn a portion of trading fees generated by swaps.

## Risks

- Impermanent loss
- Slippage during large trades
