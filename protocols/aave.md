# Aave

Aave is a decentralized lending protocol that allows users to lend and borrow cryptocurrencies.

Users deposit assets into liquidity pools and earn interest.

Borrowers can take loans by providing collateral.

## Key Features

- overcollateralized loans
- variable and stable interest rates
- flash loans

## How It Works

1. Users deposit assets into the protocol.
2. The assets are pooled for lending.
3. Borrowers can take loans by locking collateral.
