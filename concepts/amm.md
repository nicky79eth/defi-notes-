# Automated Market Maker (AMM)

Automated Market Makers allow users to trade tokens without a traditional order book.

Instead, trades happen against a liquidity pool.

## Formula

Most AMMs use the constant product formula:

x * y = k

Where:

x = reserve of token A  
y = reserve of token B  
k = constant value

## Advantages

- permissionless trading
- continuous liquidity
- no order book

## Risks

- impermanent loss
- price slippage
